## Overview

Founded in June 2022, Sandy Consultancy is a mid-sized IT outsourcing firm that partners with businesses to deliver technology talent, managed services, and digital transformation programmes. From its early days with a handful of consultants and clients, the company scaled rapidly to 50 professionals placed across 10 client engagements — navigating the realities of contract renewals, client exits, and a continuously evolving workforce. Today, Sandy Consultancy operates as a lean, delivery-focused organisation where people, projects, and client relationships sit at the heart of every business decision.

## Key Facts

- Workforce and client base have been dynamic — employees have joined and left over time.
- Client relationships have not all been continuous — some clients have been terminated.
- The business operates with a real-world consultancy model where headcount and client count fluctuate.
